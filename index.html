Privacy Policy for Pulse

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

Introduction

Pulse ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Mood Entries: When you create mood entries, we collect the mood emoji, song name, artist name, and your personal description of your feelings.
  • Device Information: We may collect device identifiers and operating system information.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the App, including features used and time spent.
  • Spotify Integration: When you search for music through Spotify, we collect search queries and track information. This data is processed through our secure backend servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • Enable mood tracking and music discovery features
  • Personalize your experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance the App
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • All mood entries are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
  • Your data is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync
  • You have full control over your data and can delete entries at any time

3.2 Spotify Integration

  • Spotify API calls are processed through our secure backend (Vercel)
  • Your Spotify credentials are never stored locally
  • Search queries are sent to Spotify's servers according to their Privacy Policy

3.3 Security Measures

  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • API credentials are stored securely in environment variables
  • We do not store sensitive personal information on public servers

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Spotify

  • The App integrates with Spotify's API to provide music search functionality
  • Spotify's Privacy Policy governs their data collection and use
  • Visit Spotify's Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

We retain your mood entries for as long as you keep them on your device. You can delete any entry at any time. We do not retain data on our servers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your personal data
  • Opt-out of data collection (by not using the App)
  • Request information about how your data is used

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@pulseapp.com
  • App: Pulse

This Privacy Policy is effective as of December 15, 2025 and applies to all users of the Pulse mobile application.
